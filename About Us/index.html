So, what is it that we do?

Well, we help recycle. Bottle Depot was initially founded on the foundations of recycling and ecocity. Bottle depots, a system already set up and managed by the government was not being fully made use of and that was what caught the attention of our development and design team.

The main feature of this web application is actually the calculator. Not a simple, daily use calculator, but one that calculates how much you will save in terms of deposits and incentives. An easy to use application that will inform you and teach you more about the values of recycling is our purpose.

Our design team decided that a mini game will be the best possible solution to keep the user interested and interact with the act of recycling. By implicitly encouraging green environmental acts, we will be designing a simple matching game for users and clients to enjoy and learn.

As we implmented the Google Maps API, our development team was able to develop a page on the website where you would be able to see the nearby located bottle depots as well as detailed information on each and individual retail store. Efficient and great planning for the next trip.
